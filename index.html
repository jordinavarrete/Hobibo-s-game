    ME

    Game Rules

    • On each turn, the current player can press as many cells as they want, but only from the same level.
    • Once the player has made their selection, they must press the ENTER key to finalize their turn.
    • The game continues until only one white cell remains, and the player who ensures this outcome is declared the winner.

    Features

    • You can choose between multiplayer and singleplayer mode by clicking one of the following icons: (multiplayer) / (singleplayer).
    • In singleplayer mode , you have the option to decide who makes the first move: ME (yourself) or AI (the computer).
    • The game features a clean and minimalistic design to ensure a smooth and enjoyable user experience.
    • Simple controls make the game accessible to both beginners and experienced players.

    About Me

    Who am I?

    I'm currently a Computer Science student with a passion for problem-solving, logic-based games, and software development.

    Why did I make this project?

    I've always enjoyed playing this game during school, whether with friends or by myself. It was a simple yet engaging game that sparked my interest in strategy and logical thinking.

    When I started learning programming, I realized that this game would be a perfect project to practice my skills while also recreating something nostalgic from my childhood. This project allows me to apply concepts like user interaction, game logic, and UI/UX design in a fun and meaningful way.
